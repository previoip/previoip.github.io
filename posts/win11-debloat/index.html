Win11 Debloat

I’ve been using windows for my workstation for a long time, mainly due to lots of software just refuse to work under VMs or wine and me being well too adjusted with windows environment.

But still, microsoft forcefeeding their users with bloated unnecessary features (copilot) had me barfing just looking at the launch icons.

It is a friendly reminder that you can too remove (or at least temporarily) these features with few regedit shenanigans. But sometimes editing registry manually is a bit too tedious.

That’s why I want to highlight Win11Debloat project by Raphire (github).

Do use at your own risk though
